The Best Movies According to Rohit

My top 3 movies of all-time.

RRR

The story of two historic revolutionaries who pursued justice in British Raj in 1920 is a compelling tale of courage, sacrifice, and unwavering commitment to the cause of independence..

Ramayana: The Legend of Prince Rama

"Ramayana: The Legend of Prince Rama" is a 1992 Indo-Japanese animated film that retells the classic Indian epic, the Ramayana which tell the story of the triumph of good over evil.

Sardar Udham

The story of Udham Singh seeking revenge on Michael O'Dwyer is a real-life tale of retribution and justice.
